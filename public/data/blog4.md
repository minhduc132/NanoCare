  Maintaining good nasal hygiene is crucial for overall respiratory health. Here are some best practices to keep your nasal passages clean and healthy.

  ## Daily Nasal Care Routine

  Establishing a daily nasal care routine can significantly improve your respiratory health and prevent common issues.

  ### Regular Cleaning
  Clean your nasal passages daily using saline solution or nasal rinse products to remove allergens and irritants.

  ### Proper Technique
  Use the correct technique when performing nasal rinsing to ensure effectiveness and safety.

  ### Consistent Schedule
  Maintain a consistent schedule for nasal care to maximize the benefits and prevent respiratory problems.
